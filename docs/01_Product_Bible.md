帮助每个人提前留下最后想说的话，

---

## Vision

---

# 2. Brand Positioning

Forever Alive is not a digital will.

Forever Alive is not a funeral service.

Forever Alive is not a legal product.

Forever Alive is an emotional communication platform.

It helps people leave messages,
videos,
voices,
and memories,
which will be delivered
to the people they love
when they are no longer able to do so.

Some words

I'm sorry.

I love you.

I'm proud of you.

Forever Alive exists
to protect those words.
